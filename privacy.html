Duplicate Tabs Pro Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 6, 2026

1. Overview & Commitment

Duplicate Tabs Pro (the "Extension") is a browser productivity utility developed by Infiax Labs. We prioritize user privacy and data security. Because our system runs entirely locally on your device, we are proud to declare a strict zero-data-collection policy.

2. Data Collection Practices

The Extension operates entirely within your local browser context.

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, request, or store your name, email, payment data, or credentials.
  • No Browsing History Tracking: We do not log, track, or save the websites you visit, the tabs you duplicate, or the duration of your browsing sessions.
  • No Server Transmission: No details from your browsing activity are ever transmitted to external servers, API endpoints, or third-party networks.

3. Purpose of Browser Permissions

To perform its core features, the Extension requests minimal browser permissions. These are used strictly locally:

  • storage: Used to save your UI preference (Light Mode vs. Pure Black Dark Mode) locally on your device. This guarantees that your selected theme is remembered between browser launches.
  • activeTab: Used to duplicate the currently selected webpage when you trigger the action (either by clicking the popup button or pressing the hotkey). Access is localized and temporary, ensuring your general browsing history is never exposed.

4. Data Sharing and Transfer

Since we do not collect any user data, we do not sell, trade, rent, or transfer any information to third parties under any circumstances.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to align with updated browser security configurations. Any updates will be pushed directly to our GitHub repository.
